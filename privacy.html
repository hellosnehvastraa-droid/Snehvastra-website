Privacy Policy

Your trust is our finest thread. We protect your data like our master artisans protect ancient techniques.

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, email, phone, shipping address (during checkout)
  • Payment details (processed securely by Razorpay/Stripe - we don't store card info)
  • Size preferences and order history

Automatic Collection

  • IP address, browser type, device info
  • Cookies for site functionality and analytics
  • Pages visited, time spent, referral sources

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process orders and payments
  • Ship products and communicate order status
  • Provide customer support and auction updates
  • Improve site functionality and personalize experience
  • Send promotional emails (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • Prevent fraud and comply with legal requirements

3. Data Protection

Security: SSL encryption on all pages. PCI DSS compliant payments. Regular security audits.

Third Parties: Only share with shipping partners (DTDC/DHL), payment processors, and legal authorities when required.

Storage: Data stored in India (AWS Mumbai) with GDPR-compliant backups.

  • Order data: 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Cookies: 30 days maximum

4. Cookies & Tracking

  • Essential: Cart functionality, login sessions
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymized IP)
  • Marketing: Facebook Pixel, Instagram retargeting

Manage cookies via browser settings or our Cookie Preferences link.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: Request copy of your data
  • Delete: Request data removal (subject to legal retention)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from emails anytime
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information

Email privacy@snehvastra.store to exercise rights.

6. Children Under 13

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents may contact us to remove data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes posted 30 days in advance. Continued use = acceptance.
