Privacy Policy

Your trust is our highest priority. We protect your personal data with the same precision we craft our garments.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to serve you with excellence:

  • Name, phone, email, and shipping address
  • Secure payment confirmation (we never store card details)
  • Site behavior for UX optimization
  • NFC scans for garment authenticity

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly for order processing, delivery communication, personalization, and brand improvements.

3. Data Sharing

We never sell your data. Limited sharing occurs only with:

  • Courier & logistics partners
  • Authorized payment gateways
  • Legal authorities when mandated

4. Security Measures

We use SSL encryption, protected infrastructure, access limitation, and periodic audits.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Essential cookies ensure core functionality. Optional analytics help us improve your shopping experience.
