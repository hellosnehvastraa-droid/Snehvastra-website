Return Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

Returns and Refunds

You may return most new, unused items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be in their original packaging and condition.

Non-returnable Items

  • Final sale or limited edition drops
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Gift cards

How to Return

To initiate a return, contact our support team at support@snehvastra.store with your order details. We’ll guide you through the return process.

Refunds

Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days after we receive your returned item. All refunds will be made to your original payment method.

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. To exchange an item, please return it and place a new order for the desired product.
