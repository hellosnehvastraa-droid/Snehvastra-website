Return Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

Returns and Refunds

You may return most new, unused items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be in their original packaging.

Non-returnable Items

  • Final sale items or limited drops
  • Items that have been worn or altered
  • Gift cards

How to Return

To return an item, please contact our support at support@snehvastra.store with your order details. We will provide return instructions.

Refunds

Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned item. Refunds will use the original payment method.

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. Please return the item and place a new order.
