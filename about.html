Our story & craft

Snehvastra — built for the drop

Snehvastra blends city rhythm and careful craft. We release limited capsule drops that highlight materials, detail, and a timeless attitude — designed for those who move fast and care deeply about the pieces they wear.

Our Story

Born from late-night design sessions and long walks through market lanes, Snehvastra started as a conversation between technique and the city. Each capsule is curated with respect for material, pattern, and silhouette — made to be worn confidently and passed on.

We design in small batches, iterate quickly, and refine with hands-on sampling to ensure every piece feels intentional.

Our Mission

We connect with the new generation through meaningful drops that celebrate identity. Our mission is to make limited pieces that people remember — not just for a season, but for the story they carry.

Sustainability is considered within our supply choices, small runs, and packaging decisions — a slow approach inside a fast moment.

How Drops Work

We plan each drop as a short runway — announcement, release window, and a final close. Drops are promoted across channels, and once a capsule closes, the pieces are archived until the next release.
