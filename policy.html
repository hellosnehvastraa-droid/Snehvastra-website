1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to deliver exceptional craftsmanship and service:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone, address) for order fulfillment
  • Payment information processed securely by trusted gateways
  • Browsing behavior to improve your shopping experience
  • NFC scan data to share garment stories

2. How We Use Your Data

Order Processing

Process and ship your Snehvastra pieces with precision.

Communication

Order updates, auction alerts, craftsmanship stories.

Personalization

Recommend sizes and styles based on your preferences.

3. Data Sharing

We never sell your data. Limited sharing occurs only with:

  • Shipping partners for delivery
  • Payment processors (Stripe, Razorpay)
  • Legal authorities when required

4. Security Measures

Your data is protected with enterprise-grade security:

  • GDPR & DPDP Act compliance
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure hosting with Cloudflare protection

We use essential cookies for cart functionality and optional analytics cookies to improve our craft.
